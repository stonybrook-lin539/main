Write each one of the following expressions as a formula with the addition operator \(+\).

\[ 5 - \sum_{1 \leq i \leq 3} i \]

\[ \sum_{i = 1} i + i + i \]

\[ 5 - \sum_{1 \leq i \leq 3} i = 5 - (1 + 2 + 3) \]

\[ \sum_{i = 1} i + i + i = 1 + 1 + 1 \]

Suppose that \(\mathcal{S} \mathrel{\mathop:}=\left \{ \left \{ 0,3 \right \}, \left \{ 9,23 \right \}, \left \{ 2,9 \right \} \right \}\) as before. What is the value of the formula below, assuming that \(i \oplus j = ij\) if \(i \geq j\) and \(ji\) otherwise (for example, \(8 \oplus 7 = 87\) and \(3 \oplus 15 = 153\)).

\[ \prod_{S \in \mathcal{S}} \bigoplus_{n \in S} n \]

\[\begin{align*} \prod_{S \in \mathcal{S}} \bigoplus_{n \in S} n &= \bigoplus_{n \in \left \{ 0,3 \right \}} n \times \bigoplus_{n \in \left \{ 9,23 \right \}} n \times \bigoplus_{n \in \left \{ 2,9 \right \}} n \\ &= 0 \oplus 3 \times 9 \oplus 23 \times 2 \oplus 9 \\ &= 30 \times 239 \times 92 \\ &= 659,640 \end{align*}\]
